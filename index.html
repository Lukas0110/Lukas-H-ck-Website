Westendorf - Austria

This site is focused on a small village in the Austrian Alps. There are a large variety of activities available here, which are suatible for all seasons of the year.

The village is situated roughly 16Km to the west of Kitzbuhel. There are train connections to iconic cities such as Saltzburg and Vienna, making it easily acessible via many other modes of transport.
Driving is also a viable option as the village lies next to 170 (which can be seen on the bottom of the image above).
